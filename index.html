Send a support request

To help us solve issues quickly, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15) and iOS version
  • What happened, what you expected, and any error text you saw
  • Screenshots if the issue is visual or blocking you

Quick answers

  • App not syncing across devices? Make sure you are signed into the same iCloud account and iCloud Drive is enabled. Open Bloomtrace, pull down to refresh, and wait a minute for CloudKit to finish syncing.
  • Accidentally deleted data? If you use iCloud, your entries may be recoverable from iCloud backups. Contact us and we will guide you through the steps that apply to your device.

Still stuck? Email us and we will provide specific steps for your setup.

Feedback & feature requests

Data & privacy

Bloomtrace stores your collection privately on your device and in your iCloud account when sync is on. Read the full Privacy Policy for details, or email us if you want help removing data from CloudKit.

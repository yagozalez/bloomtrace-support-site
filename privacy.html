Bloomtrace Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Introduction

Bloomtrace respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email address, or other details you choose to provide when creating an account.
  • Usage data: Interactions within the app. We use this to improve Bloomtrace.
  • CloudKit data: Plant information, photos, and care events you save to iCloud.
  • Analytics: Aggregated metrics collected via Apple and third-party services (update this section to reflect the tooling you actually use).

How We Use Information

  • To deliver and improve Bloomtrace.
  • To sync your data securely between devices.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To monitor usage trends and fix issues.

Data Storage & Security

Your personal data stays on your devices and in your iCloud account unless you explicitly share it with us. We use industry-standard safeguards to protect any data we process.

Your Choices

  • Access and update your information within the app.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us at privacy@yourdomain.com.
  • Turn off analytics in Settings if your app provides that option.

Children's Privacy

Bloomtrace is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we have personal data for a child under 13, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out at privacy@yourdomain.com.
